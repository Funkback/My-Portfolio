Noussonic

Noussonic is a company we collaborated with to create an immersive and interactive auditory experience, where one navigates through multiple levels and encounters various environments within a 3D space.

Backwards Speaker

A collaborative project with Kreativum, a natural science museum. Our mission was to conceive and construct an interactive installation that allows you to speak into a microphone and experience the playback of your voice in reverse, all while receiving captivating visual feedback.

Interactive sound system

An interactive sound system that utilizes Arduino, Pure Data, and ultrasonic sensors to play music and notes by positioning the hand at various distances from the sensors.
